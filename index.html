About Me

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in CS and a minor in Mathematics in the sprint of 2017. Since then, I have been working as a Software Engineer at OnLogic in the E-Commerce space.

My free time is mostly consumed by experimenting with cooking and baking. To me, cooking is a relatively efficient way of practicing problem solving skills, time management, and adapting to changes in the environment.
